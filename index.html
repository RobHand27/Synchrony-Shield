How It Works

Our browser extension uses pattern matching and DOM traversal to identify and redact sensitive data without sending information to external AI systems

Step One

Visit the website to download the Synchrony Shield extension

Step Two

Input the text into the LLM or other text field for scanning

Step Three

The extension identifies sensitive content through custom inputs

Uses regex patterns to scan input text for patterns containing PII

Utilizes Dom traversals to search HTML fields to blur and replace content

Delivers informative HTML pop-up and filters text completely

About Synchrony Shield

Synchrony Shield addresses a critical challenge facing organizations today. While many teams turn to LLM technology for data processing, the risk of leaking sensitive and confidential information through external AI systems has become a major concern.

Our team developed Synchrony Shield as a secure alternative that doesn't rely on external AI systems. Instead, we use advanced regex pattern matching, DOM traversal, and custom phrase detection to protect sensitive data locally within your browser, ensuring your confidential information never leaves your control.

With Synchrony Shield, organizations can confidently work with sensitive data while maintaining complete control over information security. Our solution focuses on user education and pattern-based protection, making it ideal for environments where data confidentiality is paramount.
